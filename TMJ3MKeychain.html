CNC Keychain

Find image to trace.
 Comments: I chose the glyph that represents Roshar from The Stormlight Archive by Brandon Sanderson, I don't think I realized how square the glyph would be and it ended up taking very little space.

Trace the image in fusion and expost to DXF.
 Comments: This was fun and very relaxing; I was lucky in that the image I chose was symmetric so I only ended up having to trace one half.

Using a USB, import the DXF file onto a Tormach and turn it into a g-code file:
 Comments:

Run the g-code into an already prepared keychain.
 ➢ Keychain was cut from aluminum and the corners were rounded on the big CNC
 ➢ The keychain's edges and sides were then sanded, the hole deburred
 Comments: I have many memories on sanding keychains…

Zero out the CNC so that the image is centered on to the keychain.


Flip the keychain over and engrave text on that side.
 Comments: The text I engraved was Life before death. This is part of a longer quote from The Stormlight Archive, "Life before death. Strength before weakness. Journey before destination." These words are the first oath that knights radiants swear. This is also why I somewhat regret engraving Roshar (it's not even the planet I feel most attached to), when I could have engraved the glyphpair for Life before death.
